About Waste Sorter

The Waste Sorter project is designed to promote effective waste separation and recycling habits within a community.

Recycle Tips Section

A dedicated learning area where facilitators can educate members on how to properly sort waste.

  • Visual guides
  • Best practices
  • Tutorials for separating recyclables, compostables, and general waste
Waste Logs Section

A tracking space where members can log their waste collection and separation activities.

  • Monitor progress
  • Encourage accountability
  • Showcase community efforts in proper waste management
Our Goal: Support environmental education and real-world action in sustainable waste management.
