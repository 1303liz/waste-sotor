How Your Waste Gets Recycled

1. Collection

Our facilitators collect sorted waste from designated collection points. Proper sorting at this stage is critical for efficient recycling.

2. Sorting & Processing

At our recycling centers, waste is further sorted by material type and processed into raw materials for manufacturing.

3. Manufacturing

Processed materials are transformed into new products, reducing the need for virgin materials and minimizing environmental impact.
